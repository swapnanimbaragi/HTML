1.Salaar Part 1 Ceasefire - 113.2 million & BOX OFFICE COLLECTION: ₹ 1200 crore 😱

"Salaar: Part 1 – Ceasefire" is a Telugu-language epic action thriller film directed by Prashanth Neel. The film stars Prabhas and Prithviraj Sukumaran in the lead roles, with an ensemble cast including Shruti Haasan, Jagapathi Babu, Bobby Simha, Sriya Reddy, Ramachandra Raju, John Vijay, Easwari Rao, Tinnu Anand, Devaraj, Brahmaji, and Mime Gopi. The story is set in the fictional dystopian city-state of Khansaar, where constitutional monarchy still exists. It follows the friendship between Deva, the exiled prince, and Varadha, the current prince, as they navigate a coup d'état planned by their father's ministers and relatives. The film's release was highly anticipated, and it received positive reviews from critics. Wikipedia
